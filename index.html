FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is ElecticaOS? +

ElecticaOS is an AI-powered battery intelligence platform that sits between the charger and the battery. It uses patented adaptive charging algorithms to extend battery life by up to 40%, while providing real-time diagnostics at cell level.

How does adaptive charging work? +

Unlike standard CC-CV (constant current, constant voltage) charging, our AI continuously adjusts charging parameters based on real-time cell chemistry, temperature, and degradation state. This minimizes stress on the battery while maintaining optimal charge times.

Is the technology validated? +

Yes. Our results are peer-reviewed and published in IEEE. We demonstrated 67.2% capacity retention after 3,000 cycles (vs. 45% for standard CC-CV) and a 37.5% increase in total cycle life.

What vehicle types are supported? +

ElecticaOS supports E-Buses, Electric Cars (E4W), E-Rickshaws (E3W), E-Scooters (E2W), High-Payload Drones, and grid-scale energy storage systems. The platform is chemistry-agnostic and works with LFP, NMC, and other battery types.

How is ElecticaOS deployed? +

ElecticaOS can be deployed as a SaaS platform for fleet operators, or licensed directly to OEMs and charge point operators for embedding into their hardware and software stack.

What data do you need from our fleet? +

We integrate via standard protocols (OCPP for chargers, CAN bus for vehicles). Minimum data includes voltage, current, temperature, and SoC readings. Historical data helps train better models, but we can start with real-time data alone.

What is the typical ROI timeline? +

Most fleet operators see ROI within 3-6 months through reduced degradation and optimized charging costs. Use our ROI calculator above for a personalized estimate based on your fleet parameters.
